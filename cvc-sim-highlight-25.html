From 2023 to Q3 2024, more than 25 programmes were conducted within the local communities, such as:

PETRONAS Brazil's Trilha Empreendedora project empowers over 5,000 underprivileged students with learning opportunities in financial education, labor market insights, and entrepreneurship. With development classes and mentorship for public school students, this initiative builds pathways to knowledge, fosters career readiness, and provides a platform for volunteers to support student growth through group and online mentoring.

In Canada, our bursary program 296 Hug × 224 Hug in Lights College and the University of Northern British C program, provides upskilling opportunities for nurses from northern and Indigenous communities. This initiative addresses healthcare workforce shortages, improves care in underserved regions, and supports professional growth among Indigenous and northern students. By investing in nursing education, we contribute to the personal advancement of nurses while bolstering healthcare access and resilience in these communities.

The AgroDucation Programme brings agricultural education to Klang Valley schools like SMK Menjalara, inspiring students with hands-on training in sustainable farming. Through this program, students gain practical skills in eco-friendly agriculture, learn the benefits of healthy eating, and explore ways to generate income from produce. AgroDucation enriches students’ understanding of sustainability and prepares them with valuable life skills.
